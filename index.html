Welcome to my website

I am a theoretical computational physicist in the field of High Energy Physics. In my work I use computers to provide high-precision quantitative predictions of physical quantities, in those regimes where analytic solutions are out of reach.

  • Current employer: University of Bern
  • Job title: Postdoctoral Researcher
  • Education: PhD in Theoretical Physics

My main areas of interest are:

  • Quantum Field Theories on the lattice, QCD phenomenology
  • Machine Learning for scientific applications, Physics-Informed Neural Networks
  • Markov chain Monte Carlo, Bayesian algorithms
